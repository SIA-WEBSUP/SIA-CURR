AA and Al-Anon invite you to this annual event where groups hold workshops on areas and topics of interest to our fellowships and in our programs of recovery.

We'll have a virtual fellowship room all day long to congregate during breakfast, lunch and for the big meeting. This space will also remain open for fellowship during and in between workshops.

Each workshop will begin on the hour and run NO MORE THAN 50 minutes to allow for the turnover of the meeting space.

BREAKFAST 8am - 9am
9am 10am 11am
LUNCH 12pm - 1pm
1pm 2pm 3pm
BIG MEETING 3pm - 4:30pm

Your group is needed to host a workshop!! Please submit your Workshop Topic Ideas by the Aug 24th deadline. Click here for more info.

We need Tech Savvy volunteers to help run the Fellowship Room and the Workshops. If you are familiar with Zoom (and especially if you have experience with the host features), please email the Share-A-Thon Chair at share@suffolkny-aa.org

To learn how you can help further, please join us for the Share-A-Thon Committee Planning Meeting

We are actively soliciting ideas for this year's workshops!!

If your group would like to host a workshop this year, email the Share-A-Thon Chair at share@suffolkny-aa.org with the following info:
  • Group Name
  • Contact Name
  • Contact Email
  • Three Topic Ideas
