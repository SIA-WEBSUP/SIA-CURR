MARCH 2004                                                                                                                                            PATCHOGUE, NY WWW.SUFFOLKNY-AA.ORG TIPS ON MAKING 12TH STEP CALLS
WHEN A 12TH STEP CALL IS RECIEVED, WE BEGIN WITH THE ASSUMPTION THAT ANOTHER HUMAN BEINGS' LIFE IS AT STAKE, LITERALLY. THIS MEANS THAT, WITHOUT DELAY, THIS CALL IS TO BE ANSWERED AT ONCE...

1ST- ARRANGE FOR ANOTHER AA MEMBER TO GO WITH YOU.
2ND- HAVE A QUIET TIME, READ CHAPTER 7 IN THE BIG BOOK.
3RD- MAINTAIN ANONYMITY
4TH- TALK TO THE PROSPECT ALONE, IF POSSIBLE. (THAT IS WITHOUT HIS FAMILY OR FRIENDS THERE)
5TH- CONGRATULATE HIM ON WANTING TO DO SOMETHING ABOUT HIS DRINKING.
6TH- GIVE HIM SOME AA LITERATURE.
7TH- NOTE WELL WHAT THE BIG BOOK SAYS AT THE BOTTOM OF PAGE 94:
"ON YOUR FIRST VISIT, TELL HIM ABOUT THE FELLOWSHIP OF ALCOHOLICS ANONYMOUS". IF HE SHOWS INTEREST, LEND HIM YOUR COPY OF THE BOOK.
8TH- EACH OF YOU TELL HIM "WHAT YOU USED TO BE LIKE, WHAT HAPPENED, AND WHAT YOU ARE LIKE NOW".
9TH- IF HE WANTS TO TALK LET HIM.
10TH- AT THE TOP OF PAGE 95, IT ADVISES, "GIVE HIM A CHANCE TO THINK IT OVER". SOMETIMES A MAN IS ANXIOUS TO PROCEED AT ONCE, AND YOU MAY BE TEMPTED TO ENCORAGE THAT. THIS SOMETIMES IS A MISTAKE. IF HE HAS TROUBLE LATER, HE IS LIKELY TO SAY YOU RUSHED HIM. IF HE IS SINCERELY INTERESTED AND WANTS TO SEE YOU AGAIN, ASK HIM TO READ THE BOOK IN THE INTERIM. (ASK HIM TO READ THE FIRST 164 PAGES.) AFTER DOING THAT, (READING THE BOOK), HE MUST DECIDE BY HIMSELF IF HE WANTS TO GO ON OR NOT.
11TH- WHEN YOU ARE READY TO LEAVE, TELL HIM YOU WILL CALL ON HIM THE FOLLOWING DAY IF HE WANTS, AND HE WILL HAVE HAD TIME TO READ THE FIRST 164 PAGES, OR HAD TIME TO THINK ABOUT OUR CONVERSATION.
12TH- NOTE THAT THE SECOND PARAGRAPH ON PAGE 96 SAYS" SUPPOSE YOU ARE NOW MAKING YOUR SECOND VISIT TO A MAN. HE HAS READ THIS VOLUME, AND HE IS PREPARED TO GO THROUGH WITH THE TWELVE STEPS OF THE PROGRAM OF RECOVERY". AT THIS POINT YOU REVIEW THE 12 STEPS WITH HIM, AND ARRANGE TO BRING HIM TO YOUR GROUP MEETING. IF HE DOES NOT WANT TO GO ON, OR FEELS THAT HE CAN DO IT SOME OTHER WAY, PICK UP YOUR COPY OF THE BIG BOOK AND INVITE HIM TO CALL ON YOU AGAIN IF HE CHANGES HIS MIND AND DECIDES THAT AA CAN BE OF HELP.
