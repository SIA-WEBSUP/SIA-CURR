Meeting Place
The Group reps have decided to hold one SIA business Meeting per quarter in Riverhead or its environs.  If anyone from the area can suggest a suitable meeting place, capable of holding 100 people and costing about $50 per night, please call the SIA office. Office Help
Volunteers are needed to take shifts at the SIA office between 9:00PM and 3:00 PM, except Sundays, and to fill-in in emergencies. Office work involves answering the phones and light paperwork.  Volunteers also  help with committee work.  Anyone who is interested contact office manager, Walter S., at 654-1150.
