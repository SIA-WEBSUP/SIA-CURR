SIA Hotline

The AA Hotline needs you and especially your group to sign up to answer the phones.

Your group does not need to take all 14 shifts in the week. We can help find another group to share the week or fill in any gaps in the coverage. We are also looking for individual volunteers.

You do not need a physical Hotline Book to work the hotline — there is an online version available here. If you or your group do need (a) physical book(s), they can be picked up at the SIA Office or other pick-up / drop-off arrangements can be made.

The shifts run from 8am to 8pm starting on Sundays.

Any and all please inquire by emailing phones@suffolkny-aa.org.

The Hotline Chair will be away and unavailable in the month of January. We urgently need the 6 weeks from Dec 27th – Feb 6th filled. Please consider being the Hand of AA extended to a newcomer, or any alcoholic still suffering, reaching out for help.

Finding Meetings
using
Meeting Guide App

The AA Meeting Guide App is a free of charge meeting finder app for iOS and Android that provides meeting information from hundreds of intergroups and other A.A. service entities, nation-wide, in an easy-to-access format.

Because we are limited in what information we can push to the meeting guide app, COVID Reopen Status Unknown meetings and Temporarily Closed meetings will be displayed similarly — both the group name and meeting location will be displayed using strikethrough text. (e.g. THE STATUS UNKNOWN GROUP)
